# 008-odds-and-broadcast

## Goal
Remove odds, broadcast, affiliate, and betting-adjacent surfaces from the MVP plan so match pages stay result-focused.

## Build
1. Remove odds tabs, bookmaker entities, affiliate-link routing, broadcast modules, and related CTA planning from the required product surface area.
2. Keep competition and match pages centered on scores, status, fixtures, results, and essential events only.
3. Treat any provider capability for odds or broadcast metadata as optional and non-blocking. Do not let it shape the MVP routes, schema, or UI.
4. Remove geo-targeting, regulated betting copy, age gating, and conversion tracking from MVP acceptance criteria.
5. Remove monetization-driven layout slots from page definitions and release validation.
6. Document odds or broadcast features as future ideas rather than active delivery work.

## Done When
- No MVP route or component requires odds, broadcast, or betting modules.
- Provider selection and release readiness do not depend on non-core feeds.
- Match and competition plans stay cleanly focused on results data.
