# 009-news-hub

## Goal
Remove news and editorial work from the MVP plan so the team can focus on the matches and results product.

## Build
1. Drop the `News` landing page, article pages, and article-linked homepage placements from required route planning.
2. Remove editorial ingestion, moderation, and sponsored-content workflows from the MVP timeline.
3. Ensure homepage, competition pages, and match pages do not depend on news cards or editorial modules to feel complete.
4. Keep the information architecture centered on match discovery, not content discovery.
5. Document news as a possible later expansion only after the core scores product is stable.

## Done When
- The MVP plan no longer includes news routes or editorial tooling.
- Public pages are complete without article content.
- The roadmap reflects one product focus instead of mixing scores and publishing work.
