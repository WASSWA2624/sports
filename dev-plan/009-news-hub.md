# 009-news-hub

## Goal
Ship the Flashscore-style news hub and article system, including monetized news journeys.

## Build
1. Build the top-level `News` landing page with sport and topic grouping.
2. Build article listing modules for sport, competition, team, and homepage placements.
3. Implement article detail pages with SEO metadata, related matches, related teams, related competitions, and linked odds or CTA modules where allowed.
4. Create an ingestion or editorial workflow for article publishing, updates, takedowns, sponsored labels, and entity-linked surface placement.
5. Add lightweight editor moderation tools for article quality, entity linking, CTA placement, and inline monetization safety.
6. Ensure scores pages, competition pages, and team pages can surface news cards, promo units, and funnel CTAs inline without breaking page density.

## Done When
- News is a first-class section alongside scores.
- Articles are discoverable from homepage, competition, and team contexts.
- Editorial content and monetized news placements can be managed safely by staff.
