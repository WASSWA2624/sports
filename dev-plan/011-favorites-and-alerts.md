# 011-favorites-and-alerts

## Goal
Remove favorites, alerts, and retention loops from the MVP critical path.

## Build
1. Remove favorite actions for matches, teams, and competitions from the required homepage and match-row behavior.
2. Drop the favorites page, `My Teams` modules, and alert-subscription flows from the release plan.
3. Keep homepage ordering and competition grouping based on feed data and product defaults rather than personalization.
4. Use shareable URLs and stable query parameters for revisit support instead of account-backed retention features.
5. Remove reminder prompts, notification work, and channel-entry flows from the MVP backlog.
6. Document favorites and alerts as later enhancements only if the minimal public product proves stable and useful first.

## Done When
- No MVP acceptance criteria depend on favorites or alerts.
- The board remains uncluttered by personalization controls.
- Retention features are clearly deferred rather than partially implemented.
